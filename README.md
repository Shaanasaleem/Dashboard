📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built using Superstore sales data to analyze overall business performance, profitability, and customer trends.

The dashboard enables users to explore sales insights across regions, segments, categories, and time periods.

---

## 📈 What the Dashboard Shows

- KPI cards for:
  - Total Sales (2M)
  - Total Profit (175K)
  - Total Quantity Sold (22K)
  - Average Metric (~3.93)

- Region-wise sales distribution (West, East, Central, South)
- Segment-wise sales (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Payment mode analysis (COD, Online, Cards)
- Monthly Sales and Profit trends (Year-over-Year comparison: 2019 vs 2020)
- Category and Sub-category performance
- State-wise sales and profit visualization (map)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- West region contributes the highest share of sales (~33%), followed by East (~29%)
- Consumer segment dominates with ~48% of total sales
- COD is the most used payment method (~42%), indicating preference for offline payment
- Sales and profit show a steady increase towards the end of the year, especially in Q4
- Technology category performs strongly in terms of revenue
- Some sub-categories generate high sales but comparatively lower contribution
